Visual Algebra Game

Welcome to the Visual Algebra Game! Explore algebra through diagrams, balance models, and graphical representations in this interactive challenge. Each round presents a visual problem — study the shapes, symbols, or models carefully and determine the correct algebraic solution before the timer runs out. You earn points for every correct interpretation, with bonus points for fast and accurate thinking. If your answer is incorrect, a hint will help you understand the visual relationship behind the equation. As you progress, you will unlock more complex visual models and multi-step reasoning tasks. There is no penalty for skipping a question, but your final score reflects both understanding and efficiency. Remember: visual algebra helps you see the structure behind the symbols.
