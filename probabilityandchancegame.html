Probability and Chance Game

Welcome to the Probability and Chance Game! Test your understanding of likelihood, outcomes, and event relationships in a fun and interactive challenge. Each round presents you with a probability scenario — read it carefully, calculate the chances, and choose the correct answer before the timer runs out. You earn points for every correct solution, with bonus points awarded for accuracy and speed. If you answer incorrectly, a helpful hint will appear to guide you toward the correct reasoning. Progress through levels that introduce compound events, conditional probability, and real-world problem solving. There are no penalties for skipping a question, but your final score reflects both precision and pace. Good luck, and remember: probability is about logical thinking, not guessing!
